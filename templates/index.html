What Makes a Data Scientist Valuable?

Ever wondered what you should do to add some weight to your Data Science resume?

Many of us already have a good notion of what is important to build a strong data science career and increase our compensation. But I personally, have never seen a systematic, data based, approach to that problem.

That's why I built a model to predict the probability of a data scientist earn more than U$ 100k per year.

The model was built over Kaggle's second annual Machine Learning and Data Science Survey. You can check out here how this model was developed and deployed.
